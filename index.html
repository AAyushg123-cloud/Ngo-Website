About Us

Suvida Foundation is awarded by Global Sustainability Awards 2024 it is serving soceity since 1995.

Our Beginning

Suvida Mahila Mandal, established on September 8,1995, is a non-profit organization working to impart education among the financially challenged sections to help them realize parity in education and strenght of littile minds in building a promising future. the organizations has provisions of student internships, student mentorships, and the scope to volunteer.

Our Mission

To inspire studends,help them innovate and let them integrate to build the next generation humankind.
To run food donation and awareness campaign in rural region

Our Vision

To build Next Generation Enterpreneur, by providing them a Skill-Based Education.To provide Internship, Training and Workshops and Quality Education all over the World.

Internship
